Auto update

Function

Update Tablacus Explorer after a period of inactivity.

Options

  • Day

Check for updates again later(Day) Default: Once in 1 day. Minimum: 1 day

  • Background

Check for updates in idle state(Minute or hour) Default: 10 minutes in idle state. Minimum: 1 minute

  • Auto

Omit confirmation dialog
