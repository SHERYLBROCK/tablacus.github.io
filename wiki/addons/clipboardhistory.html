Clipboard history

Function

Record a few cut and copy operations, and let you choose to paste using a context menu.

Note

Steps to use:

  1. Do a few cut and copy operations.
  2. Right click an empty space of the destination folder.
  3. Choose the source to paste.
  4. Choose to copy, move or create shortcut.

You can customize the maximum number of cut and copy operations to record. The default is 15.
