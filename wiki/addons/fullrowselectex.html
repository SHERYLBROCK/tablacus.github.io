Full Row Select Ex

Function

Temporarily disable full row selection when the mouse pointer is in the folder contents area

Note

Use this add-on if you

  1. have opted “single click to open (hover to select)” in “Folder Options” of Windows control panel
  2. have opted “full row select” in “Tools > Options > List > Style”
  3. often need to hover beside an item to see its tip popup but do not want to affect the selection you have made Usually this add-on is not needed.
