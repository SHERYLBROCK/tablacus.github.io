Systray

Function

Opt to show TE icon in Windows System Tray (aka Notification Area)

  1. when TE is minimized
  2. when TE is closed by clicking the “close” button in the title bar

Note

  • You can also opt to show the icon whenever TE is running.
  • When this add-on is enabled, you need to use the “Close application” menu option to exit TE completely.
